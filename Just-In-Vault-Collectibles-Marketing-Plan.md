# Just-In Vault Collectibles Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary

Just-In Vault Collectibles is an online collectibles business specializing in diecast cars, trading cards, Funko Pops, action figures, and pop culture collectibles. The goal is to build a trusted collector-focused brand while growing sales through eBay, social media, and content marketing.

---

# Business Overview

## Business Name
Just-In Vault Collectibles

## Mission Statement

To help collectors find amazing collectibles while providing excellent customer service, fast shipping, and a trustworthy buying experience.

## Primary Sales Channels

- eBay
- Facebook Marketplace
- Instagram
- TikTok
- Future Website

---

# Target Market

## Primary Audience

Collectors aged 25-55 interested in:

- Hot Wheels
- Mini GT
- Kaido House
- Time Micro
- Funko Pops
- Pokémon Cards
- Sports Cards
- Action Figures

## Secondary Audience

Parents and gift buyers purchasing collectibles.

---

# SWOT Analysis

## Strengths

- Strong knowledge of collectibles
- Passionate collector community
- Wide product categories
- Personalized customer service

## Weaknesses

- Limited brand awareness
- Small marketing budget
- Dependence on third-party marketplaces

## Opportunities

- Short-form video content
- YouTube collecting content
- Email marketing
- Collector communities
- Search engine optimization

## Threats

- Large retailers
- Competition from established sellers
- Market fluctuations
- Counterfeit products

---

# Marketing Objectives

## Goal 1

Increase eBay sales by 25%.

## Goal 2

Reach 1,000 social media followers.

## Goal 3

Establish a recognizable collectibles brand.

## Goal 4

Build a repeat customer base.
